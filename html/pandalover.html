Black & White & Asian, I am a panda.
May is a good seacon to see adult pandas.
Since the weather is not too hot,
pandas love play outside and move a lot.
and since summer holidays haven't start,
you can avoid the traffic and crowd.
Summer is difficult to evaluate.
On one hand, it is so hot.
Pandas don't like hot.
They probably only go outside in early morning.
And laying down for the rest of the day.
HOWEVER!!!
You got to see new-born panda babies!
Pandas are usually born from June to August.
And newborn panda babies are pinky and tiny!
Since scientifically they are all premature,
they always live in the infant incubator.
They will open their eyes to see the world in two months.
